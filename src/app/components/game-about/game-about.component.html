About

What do we want to achieve?

We wanted to create an easy way to search and find a persons favourite game and give the current cheapest deals available. Save your pockets by searching for current deals on your Favourite Games!!


How does it work?

As you search on our website, we are connected to the lovely CheapShark API to find the best and cheapest deals online for any beloved game that you are interested in They search high and low for those cheap Prices!! Use the wishlist button to save your game for future deals if the game currently is too pricey for your liking!!
